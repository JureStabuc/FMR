Goalless draw at King Power Stadium

The match at King Power Stadium was nothing for a goal hungry crowd. Probably most of the 31563 fans left the stadium disappointed as neither of the teams were able to put their players on the scoresheet. Leicester Southampton 0:0.

Nothing has changed for Leicester as they kept their 12th position with 8 points. Away fans won't be pleased when returning home as Southampton lost 1 place, dropping to 10th position with 9 points.
