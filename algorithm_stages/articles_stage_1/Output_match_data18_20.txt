Bournemouth held to Vitality Stadium draw by Arsenal

Home fans were delighted when Charlie Daniels scored the first goal in the match. He netted the ball in the 16th minute with an assistance by Junior Stanislas. Callum Wilson scored on the satisfaction of home fans in 21st minute. Bournemouth was 2:0 ahead of Arsenal. After an assist by Charlie Daniels Ryan Fraser shot Bournemouth in advance, leading 3:0. Arsenal fans got a bit hope when Olivier Giroud assisted Alexis Sánchez. It was the 70 minute and the score was 3:1. After an assist by Olivier Giroud Lucas Pérez scored for Arsenal, lowering the score to 3:2. Olivier Giroud scored the last goal of the match in 92nd minute who was assisted by Granit Xhaka. Bournemouth Arsenal 3:3.
