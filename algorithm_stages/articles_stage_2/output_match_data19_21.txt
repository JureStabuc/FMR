Burnley defeated Southampton at Turf Moor

The match at Turf Moor was decided in the 78th minute. The only goal in the match was scored by Joey Barton. Although seeing only one goal, 20254 home fans were delighted as Burnley secured 3 points. Burnley Southampton 1:0. 

Burnley advanced with this win from 12th place to 10th place with 26 points. Southampton on the other hand have 24 points and dropped from 10th to 13th place.
