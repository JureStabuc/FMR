Arsenal win over Burnley with 2 : 1

59955 fans saw the first goal in the 59th minute. Shkodran Mustafi scored after an assistance by Mesut Özil. Andre Gray netted the ball in the 93rd minute to the delight of away fans and drew the score to 1:1. Alexis Sánchez scored the last goal of the match in 98th minute. Arsenal Burnley 2:1. 

Arsenal advanced with this win from 4th place to 2nd place with 47 points. Burnley on the other hand have 26 points and dropped from 10th to 13th place.
