Man Utd held to Old Trafford draw by Arsenal

Home fans were delighted when Juan Mata scored the first goal in the match. He netted the ball in the 69th minute with an assistance by Ander Herrera. Olivier Giroud scored the last goal of the match in 89th minute who was assisted by Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain. Man Utd Arsenal 1:1. 

The draw didn't change anything for teams as both kept their positions from previous round. Man Utd are 6th with 19 points and Arsenal can be found on 4th place with 25 points.
