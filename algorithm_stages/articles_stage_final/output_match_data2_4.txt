Man City defeated Man Utd at Old Trafford

Home fans were not delighted when Kevin De Bruyne scored the first goal in the match. He netted the ball in the 15th minute with assistance from Kelechi Iheanacho. Kelechi Iheanacho led Man City into 0:2 advance in the 36th minute. Zlatan Ibrahimovic scored the last goal of the match in 42nd minute. Man Utd Man City 1:2. 

Man City kept the 1st place from the last round with the win, having 12 points. Man Utd dropped with this defeat to 4th place with 9 points.
