Jürgen Klopp's side fails to win over West Ham in a 2 : 2 draw

Adam Lallana opened the scoring for Liverpool in the 5th minute after an assist by Sadio Mané. Home fans were dissapointed when Dimitri Payet scored and gave hope to West Ham. Score was drew to 1:1. Michail Antonio fired Liverpool in the 39th minute into 1:2 advance. Divock Origi settled the score of the match in 48th minute. Liverpool 2 West Ham 2. 

The draw didn't change anything for teams as both kept their positions from previous round. Liverpool are 3rd with 31 points and West Ham can be found on 17th place with 13 points.
