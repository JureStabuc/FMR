Arsenal defeated Burnley at Emirates Stadium

Arsenal lead came in the 59th minute when Mesut Özil assisted the goal for Shkodran Mustafi. Burnley scored in the 93rd minute with Andre Gray's successfull finish. With this goal Burnley drew the score to 1:1. Alexis Sánchez scored the last goal of the match in 98th minute. Arsenal Burnley 2:1. 

Arsenal advanced with this win from 4th place to 2nd place with 47 points. Burnley on the other hand have 26 points and dropped from 10th to 13th place.
