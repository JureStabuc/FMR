Goalless draw at Anfield

The match at Anfield was nothing for a goal hungry crowd. Probably most of the 52769 fans left the stadium disappointed as neither of the teams were able to put their players on the scoresheet. Liverpool Man Utd 0:0.

Nothing has changed for Liverpool as they kept their 4th position with 17 points. Away fans won't be pleased when returning home as Man Utd lost 1 place, dropping to 7th position with 14 points.
